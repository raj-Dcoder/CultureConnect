Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

CultureConnect ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application designed to help travelers explore Indian cities, discover cultural events, and compare ride fares. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

a) Account Information (via Google Sign-In)

  • Your name and email address from your Google account
  • Your profile photo URL

This is used to create your account and personalize your experience.

b) Location Data

  • Your device's GPS location (only when you grant permission)
  • Used to detect your current city, auto-fill ride pickup location, and determine whether you are a "local" or "visitor"

Location data is processed on-device and is not stored on our servers.

c) Usage Data (Firebase Analytics)

  • Screen views, feature usage, and app interactions
  • Crash reports and error logs (via Firebase Crashlytics)

This anonymous data helps us improve the app and fix bugs.

d) User Preferences

  • Your home city and cultural interests (selected during onboarding)
  • Your saved/bookmarked events

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalize the app experience
  • To show culturally relevant events and content
  • To compare ride fares between providers for your selected route
  • To improve app performance and fix crashes

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We use the following third-party services that may process data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting
  • Google Maps/Places SDK — Location search and ride fare estimation

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore) with security rules that ensure you can only access your own data. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view your profile data within the app
  • Deletion: You can request account deletion by contacting us
  • Location: You can revoke location permission at any time via your phone settings

6. Children's Privacy

CultureConnect is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.
